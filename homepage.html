Explanation

First you allocate a budget through paypal or credit card. Then you submit target demographic info along with an ad. After you submit that, you select target regions. Then your ad is checked by a live person who then either validates or rejects it as inappropriate. If invalidated, you will receive an email with an explanation. If accepted your ad will appear in the Current Ads section. There, you can delete target regions, or add them by clicking Update this ad and then inserting new target regions in the select target regions form. When adding, your ad will be re-reviewed for geographic suitability. After your ad campaign is over or you delete the ad, your ad will be moved to the Past Ads section where you will no longer see target regions on a map but will have access to all the information provided in the Current Ads section.
